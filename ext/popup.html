Amazon Logistics, Inc.

  • State Wage and Hour Violation 03/08/2021: Penalty Amount - $6,454,110

    • The Labor Commissioner's Office cited Green Messengers Inc. and Amazon.com Services LLC $6.4 million for wage theft violations affecting 718 workers. The Santa Ana-based contractor delivered packages for Amazon.com Services in Los Angeles, Orange and San Bernardino counties.

  • Federal Wage and Hour Violation 06/10/2021: Penalty Amount - $61,710,583

    • Following a public comment period, the Federal Trade Commission has approved a final administrative consent order against Amazon, which has agreed to pay more than $61.7 million to settle charges that it failed to pay Amazon Flex drivers the full amount of tips they received from Amazon customers over a two and a half year period.

  • Federal Wage and Hour Violation 07/23/2021: Penalty Amount - $13,500,000

    • Amazon agreed to pay $13.5 million to resolve multidistrict claims brought by thousands of distribution center workes not compensated for time spent in mandatory security checks. The settlement ended an 11-year-old legal battle.

Whole Foods Market, Inc. (Acquired by Amazon in 2017)

  • Federal Wage and Hour Violation 03/10/2023: Penalty Amount - $185,000

    • Whole Foods agreed to pay $185,000 to settle litigation alleging that proper compensation was not paid to New York Police Department officers who did off-duty security work for the grocery chain.
